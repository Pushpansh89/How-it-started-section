Groceries on Auto-pilot

Shop à la carte any time, but if there are a few items you like to keep on hand, subscribe to them! We also offer recurring Farm Boxes, containing curated produce and other goods, that you can customize to fit your meal plan. Subscriptions will auto-populate your cart according to the frequency you set. Skip or cancel whenever, no fees or penalties.

No Checkout Required

With our easy no-checkout process, you can add or edit your order until your cut-off deadline. At this time, you’ll be charged for whatever is in your cart, as we place the order. Your food is then picked, harvested, baked, made, or packed (often within 1 day of your delivery). At most, perishable inventory is turned over in 72 hours, generating less than 1% food waste. That way, nothing overstays its welcome on our meticulously curated shelves.

Get it Delivered

Our team of dedicated foodies assembles your box the morning of your delivery, and your personally selected groceries are delivered right to your door. You’ll get a text message when your box leaves our warehouse and another when the delivery’s been made. This text also includes a link to our Kitchen Notebook which is chock-full of delicious recipes and inspiration for how to prepare/enjoy your fresh goodies.
